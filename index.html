COUNTER: Enemy attacks first. If enemy damage is dealt, it's doubled back. If enemy uses BLOCK, COUNTER, or HEAL — no counter damage. QUICK ATK cannot be countered — too fast! Counter wastes your turn.

HEAL: +6 HP but max HP drops by 2. (e.g. 5/10 → 8/8 — healed but capped!)

BLOCK: Reduces incoming damage by 2. Great against HEAVY ATK (4 dmg → 2)!

TIE PRIORITY: Both actions happen simultaneously.

CPU DIFFICULTIES: Easy = random moves. Medium = uses smart logic. Hard = reads patterns and counters your playstyle.

ONLINE MODE: Create a room and share the 4-letter code with your opponent. Both players pick moves secretly — resolves automatically!
